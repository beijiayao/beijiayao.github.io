Part 2: Fu Zhi Jiang You (seasoned soy sauce)


Ingredients


  • 500g Soy Sauce
  • 150g Water
  • 5g Star anise
  • 5g Cinnamon Sticks
  • 3g Sani
  • 5g Black Cardamom, whole
  • 3g Bay Leaves
  • 10g ginger, whole
  • 10 g green onion, cut in half
  • 400g Brown Sugar
  • 15g Tian Miang Jiang Sauce

Directions


  1. Add water and dry seasoning to wok. Bring to a boil and reduce to a simmer.
  2. Add soy sauce and brown sugar to wok and continue to simmer. Add sweet sauce and simmer for about 2 hours.
    The sauce will get thicker and sticky.
