PartV: Zhong Shui Jian (Pork dumplings with Chili Sauce)


Ingredients


  • Fu Zhi Jiang You (seasoned soy sauce)
  • Spicy Pepper Chili Oil
  • Frozen Dumplings
  • 1/2 cup crushed garlic/li>

Directions


Bring a pot of water to a boil.
Add dumplings and return pot to a boil.
Pour a little bit of water out and add 1/2 cup cold water.
Return pot to stove top and boil again (this helps with the dumpling wrapper texture).
Repeat one more time. Remove dumpling from pot and drain excess water.
Place in bowl. Top with desired amounts of seasoned soy sauce.
pepper/chili oil, and crushed garlic. Serve as is or mixed.
