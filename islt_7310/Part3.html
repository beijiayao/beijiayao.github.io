Part III: Pork Filling


Ingredients


  • 2 cups flour
  • 3/4 cup water (may need to add more)
  • 1lb pork filling

Directions


  1. Combine flour and water in mixing. Using a dough hook, need until smooth and elastic (8-10 minutes).
    The dough should not be sticky. Allow to rest for 30 minutes before using.
  2. Roll dough out into a thin sheet. Cut circles out using circular dough cutter. Dust lightly with flour if making lots at once.
  3. Fill with 2 tsp pork filling and crimp shut. Place on sheet pan lined with parchment paper. Freeze until needed.
